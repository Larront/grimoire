How should the GM interact with a scene's audio slots? Pick the layout that feels right for building and playing soundscapes.

A

Track List

Vertical list of slots, each a row with inline controls — volume slider, loop/shuffle toggles, play/pause. Compact, scannable, like a playlist or DAW track view. Familiar and efficient for both building and live use.
