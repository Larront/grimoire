A. Inline Replacement

The Spotify URI/search input is replaced entirely by a connection card. The "Connect" button triggers the OAuth flow right there. A subtle link points to Settings as an alternative.

A compact alert banner appears above the greyed-out Spotify input. Shows the input exists but isn't usable yet. Connect button right in the banner.

C. Modal Dialog

Clicking the Spotify source tab triggers a dialog explaining the connection and asking for confirmation before starting OAuth. More ceremonial, clearer about what's about to happen.

My recommendation: A (Inline Replacement) — it's the most contextual and least disruptive. The user stays in the scene flow, sees exactly why they need to connect, and can do it without a modal interrupting them.
