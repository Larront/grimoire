# The Ember Keep

A crumbling fortress on the edge of the Ashfen marshes, said to have housed a powerful arcane order before the War of Embers.

## History

The keep fell three centuries ago. Its upper towers collapsed into the marsh; the lower halls remain intact, though something that dislikes light has claimed them.

Local rumour holds that the order's library survived underground, sealed behind a puzzle lock no one has solved since.

## Notes

The herbalist [[Mira Ashvale]] warns travellers away from the western approach — she says the footing is treacherous and the warnings on the stones are not decorative.
