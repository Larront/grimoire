# Aldric Vane

A former soldier living at the edge of [[Locations/Thornhaven Village.md]]. He was part of the expedition that tried to open the sealed library four years ago. He does not talk about what they found.

## Description

Late fifties. A scar across his jaw. He drinks early in the day and keeps a long knife close.

## Personality

Stubborn and haunted. He respects directness and despises people who ask about the keep. He will lie to protect something, but not to gain something.

## Background

His family descended from coalition soldiers in [[Lore/The War of Embers.md]]. He moved to Thornhaven believing the library contained something that belonged to them. The expedition to [[Locations/The Ember Keep.md]] changed his mind about what he wanted.

## Notes

He and [[Mira]] have an uneasy truce. She knows what he found. He knows she knows. Neither has said it aloud.
