# Welcome to the Example World

This is a sample Grimoire ledger — a small fantasy locale you can wander before building your own.

Click any wikilink below to explore:

- [[The Ember Keep]] — a ruined fortress on the edge of the marshes
- [[Mira Ashvale]] — a wandering herbalist who knows every road through the Ashfen

Use the sidebar to browse notes, and the backlinks panel to see how lore connects.

When you are ready to make this world yours, choose **Make this world mine** from the sidebar.
