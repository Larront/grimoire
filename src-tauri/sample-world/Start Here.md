# Welcome to the Ashfen Vale

*A fog-wrapped corner of the world — yours to wander before you build your own.*

The Ashfen Vale is a marshland on the border of a fallen empire. A ruined fortress overlooks it from the east. A wandering herbalist knows every secret path through it. Beneath the keep's foundations, a library sealed three centuries ago still waits.

This sample world shows what a populated Grimoire ledger looks like. Explore it before building your own.

## Where to begin

- **[[Locations/The Ember Keep.md]]** — a crumbling fortress, a sealed library, and something that dislikes the light.
- **[[Characters/Mira Ashvale.md]]** — the person who knows everything the keep wants kept secret.
- **[[The Ashfen Chronicle.md]]** — a lore note with an embedded Timeline tracing three centuries of history.
- **[[Sessions/Session 1 — The Arrival.md]]** — a session log from the first night the players arrived in Thornhaven.

## What to notice

- **Sidebar folders** — notes are organised into Characters, Locations, Encounters, Sessions, and Lore.
- **Backlinks panel** — open any note and see which other notes link to it.
- **Graph** — see how all notes, maps, and stubs connect. Look for [[The Order of Embers]] — a stub note: referenced but not yet written.
- **Map** — the Ashfen Region map has pins linked back to location notes.
- **[[Mira]]** — an alias link: "Mira" resolves to [[Characters/Mira Ashvale.md]] because she has aliases set in her note.

When you are ready to make this world yours, choose **Make this world mine** from the sidebar.
