# The Ashfen Chronicle

A rough history of the Ashfen Vale, pieced together from Thornhaven's records and the accounts of [[Mira]] — who has lived near these marshes long enough to remember things she should not.

```timeline
Date: Year 0
Title: The Order Takes the Keep
The [[The Order of Embers]] finishes construction of [[Locations/The Ember Keep.md|the Ember Keep]] and establishes its arcane library in the lower halls. The Order's roads are laid across the marsh.

Date: Year 7
Title: [[Lore/The War of Embers.md|The War of Embers]] Begins
Three city-states form a coalition against the Order. They argue the library is a threat. They are not entirely wrong. The siege of [[Locations/The Ember Keep.md|the Ember Keep]] begins.

Date: Year 12
Title: The Library Is Sealed
The Order seals the library behind a puzzle lock before the final breach. Survivors scatter into the [[Lore/The Ashfen Marshes.md|Ashfen Marshes]]. The coalition appoints no one to hold the ruins.

Date: Year 312 — Present
Title: Thornhaven Endures
[[Locations/Thornhaven Village.md|Thornhaven]] survives as a market village at the marsh's edge. [[Characters/Mira Ashvale.md|Mira Ashvale]] camps near the keep and watches for movement no one else has noticed.
```
