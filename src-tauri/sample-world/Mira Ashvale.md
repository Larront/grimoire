# Mira Ashvale

aliases: Mira, the Herbalist

A weathered traveller who trades in remedies and rumours. She has mapped every road through the Ashfen and trusts no one who has not bled for their knowledge.

## Description

Mid-forties, grey-streaked hair worn back, a satchel heavy with dried plants and small glass vials. She speaks in short sentences and watches exits.

## Known Locations

She is often camped within sight of [[The Ember Keep]] — close enough to watch, far enough to run.
